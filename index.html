We've got your Southern staples made fresh, portioned, packaged and ready for you! Our LunchStacks™ are super popular, and super convenient. Once we deliver on Monday evening, just stack them in the fridge and "grab and go" all week long! The only problem you'll have is your co-workers pestering you while you eat...use your manners and share!

Collards, Pot Roast, Steamed Rice, Macaroni & Cheese, choose what you like...then simply heat and serve! And, did we mention the cornbread? Trust us on that...
